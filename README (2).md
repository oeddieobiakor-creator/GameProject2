
# UnityRewindChallenge

## Overview

This project demonstrates an enhanced "Rewind Challenge" feature in Unity, inspired by the concept of replaying and improving missed shots in sports games. It allows players to record a shot, enter a "Rewind Chamber," adjust the shot parameters, and attempt to score, with advanced replay controls, skill training, social features, and AI enhancements.

## Features

* Records player and ball data for a specific shot.
* Creates a stylized "Rewind Chamber" environment.
* Allows players to adjust the shot angle, power, and curve.
* Provides a basic UI for controlling the Rewind Challenge.
* Includes a sample scene demonstrating the feature.
* **Advanced Replay Controls & Cinematic Mode**
* **Skill Training & Performance Analysis**
* **Online & Social Features**
* **AI & Physics Enhancements**

## Requirements
* Unity 2021.3 or later
